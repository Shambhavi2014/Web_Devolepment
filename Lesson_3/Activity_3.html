HoneyBees

Honeybees are eusocial insects known for their complex social structures, vital role in pollination, and production of honey.

Bees Social Structure

Honeybees live in large colonies that consist of three types of bees:

Queen:

The sole reproductive female responsible for laying eggs and producing pheromones that regulate the hive's activities.

Workers:

Non-reproductive females that perform various tasks, including foraging for nectar and pollen, building and maintaining the hive, and caring for the queen's offspring.

Drones:

Male bees whooose primary role is to mate with a queen
