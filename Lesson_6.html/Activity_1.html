Facts About Dogs

Fossil evidence indicates that dogs were domesticated around 23,000 years ago and were the only animals domesticated during the Pleistocene Epoch (aka the Ice Age)

Dogs have up to 300 million olfactory receptors, compared to humans 5 million, enabling them to detect scents at incredibly low concentrations.
