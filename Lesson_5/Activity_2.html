Let's Learn About Our Favourite Food -


PIZZA

Common Ingredients


    Dough: Made from flour, water, yeast, salt, and sometimes olive oil. The thickness and texture vary for different varieties.


    Sauce: Most commonly tomato-based with herbs like oregano and garlic, though white sauces like béchamel are also used.


    Cheese: Mozzarella is traditional, but other cheeses like cheddar, parmesan, or vegan alternatives are popular.


    Toppings: Range from vegetables (onions, peppers, mushrooms, olives) to meats (pepperoni, sausage, chicken, ham), seafood, and even fruits (like pineapple).
